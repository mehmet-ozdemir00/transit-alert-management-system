About UnitedRelief

United Relief exists to empower communities in times of crisis. Our platform connects disaster victims with volunteers, resources, and relief organizations—helping deliver real-time aid when it's needed most.

Our Mission

We aim to make disaster response seamless and accessible. Whether you're a volunteer, a person in need, or an organization offering help, United Relief ensures you’re connected to the right support when every second counts.

Security & Privacy First

Your privacy and safety matter. We use industry-standard encryption and data protection practices to keep your personal information secure throughout your experience on our platform.

Get Involved with United Relief

Every helping hand counts. At United Relief, we are powered by a community of compassionate individuals who dedicate their time and resources to make a difference in times of crisis. By volunteering with us, you can be part of something bigger, help those in need, and bring hope where it's needed most.

Ways You Can Help

There are many ways to get involved and make a real impact. Whether you're interested in volunteering your time, donating resources, or helping us spread the word, your contribution is invaluable. Here are some ways you can help:

  • Volunteer Your Time: Join our community of volunteers to assist in emergency response efforts, provide support to victims, and help with organizing relief operations.
  • Donate Resources: Whether it's financial support or essential goods like food, water, and medical supplies, your donations help us provide immediate aid.
  • Spread the Word: Help us reach more people by sharing our mission, engaging on social media, and encouraging others to get involved.

How to Get Started

Getting started is easy! Here’s how you can begin your journey with United Relief:

  1. Sign Up: Create an account to join our volunteer network or make a donation. Our platform is easy to use and free to join.
  2. Choose Your Role: Whether you're volunteering your time or donating resources, select the way you want to contribute.
  3. Make an Impact: Once you're signed up, you'll receive notifications about upcoming opportunities and events where you can help.

Our Impact and Results

At United Relief, our mission is to make a real, measurable difference in times of crisis. We are proud to share the impact we’ve had so far. With the support of our community, partners, and volunteers, we have been able to provide life-saving assistance to those who need it the most.

Key Achievements

  • 50,000+ people helped: Through the efforts of our dedicated volunteers and partners, we have provided immediate aid to over 50,000 disaster victims.
  • 1,000+ volunteers mobilized: Volunteers have given thousands of hours to support emergency response and relief operations.
  • $2 million in donations: Thanks to generous donors, we’ve raised over $2 million to fund relief operations across the globe.
  • 500+ organizations involved: We’ve collaborated with over 500 organizations worldwide to provide essential resources and services during emergencies.

Our Ongoing Efforts

Our work doesn’t stop here. We’re constantly striving to expand our reach, improve our systems, and enhance our services to ensure we can respond effectively during the next crisis.
